Monty Hall Simulator — multi-instance test (segment 8.1 Activity 16)

Four independent widgets on one page, each defaulting to a different mode. Each must keep its own mode, sliders, strategy, running tally, and pinned simulation. Try Guided Exercise 1 on widget 1 (10 manual switch games at 3 doors); the 6-door variant on widget 2 (Monty opens 4 goat doors, switch wins 5/6 of the time); Monte Carlo at N=10000 on widget 3 (≈0.667 for switch); and N-door at 10 doors on widget 4 (≈0.9 for switch).
