Prime-factor divisibility widget — multi-instance test

Four independent widgets on one page. Each must keep its own state: answering a round on widget 1 must not advance widgets 2, 3, or 4; the per-widget score must update independently; the "Next round" and "Start over" buttons must scope to their own widget; and the block visualizations must render correctly on every widget (verifying that the per-instance setup does not collide).
